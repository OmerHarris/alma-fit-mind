Alma is not a physician, physical therapist, registered dietitian, or licensed mental health provider. Coaching is not a substitute for licensed medical care, physical therapy, dietetic treatment, or mental health treatment. You should always consult a qualified physician before beginning any exercise or nutrition program — especially if you are pregnant, have a pre-existing medical condition, are recovering from an injury or surgery, or have any concern about your ability to safely participate.

If your needs fall outside the scope of coaching — including but not limited to clinical anxiety or depression, disordered eating, an injury requiring medical clearance, or a condition requiring medical nutrition therapy — you will be referred to an appropriate licensed professional rather than coached through it.

3. Cancellation & Refund Policy

Monthly coaching subscriptions (Foundations, Transformation, Elite Mind & Body)

These are month-to-month subscriptions. You may cancel at any time; we ask for 14 days' notice where possible so we can wrap up your current coaching cycle. Cancelling stops future billing, but amounts already paid for the current billing period are not refunded, since coaching and programming for that period have been made available to you.

One-time programs (12-Week Total Transformation, Deep-Dive Session)

One-time purchases are non-refundable once your program or session has been delivered. If a genuine hardship prevents you from completing a purchased program, please contact us — transfers to a future cohort or session are considered on a case-by-case basis.

Disputes
