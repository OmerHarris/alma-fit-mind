Terms of Service, Cancellation & Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Scope of Services

Alma Fit & Mind provides online fitness, nutrition, and mindset coaching for educational and motivational purposes. Coaching is not a substitute for licensed medical care, dietetic treatment, mental health treatment, or therapy. If your needs fall outside the scope of coaching — including but not limited to clinical anxiety or depression, disordered eating, injury requiring medical clearance, or a condition requiring medical nutrition therapy — you will be referred to an appropriate licensed professional.

Before beginning any program, clients complete an intake assessment and sign an informed consent and liability waiver acknowledging the physical risks associated with exercise.

2. Cancellation & Refund Policy

Monthly coaching subscriptions (Foundations, Transformation, Elite Mind & Body)

These are month-to-month subscriptions. You may cancel at any time; we ask for 14 days' notice where possible so we can wrap up your current coaching cycle. Cancelling stops future billing, but amounts already paid for the current billing period are not refunded, since coaching and programming for that period have been made available to you.

One-time programs (12-Week Total Transformation, Deep-Dive Session)

One-time purchases are non-refundable once your program or session has been delivered. If a genuine hardship prevents you from completing a purchased program, please contact us — transfers to a future cohort or session are considered on a case-by-case basis.

Disputes

If you believe you were charged in error, contact us first at hello@almafitandmind.com so we can resolve it directly — this is almost always faster than a card dispute.

3. Payments

Payments are processed securely by Stripe. We do not store your card information. Subscription payments renew automatically each billing period until cancelled.

4. No Guarantee of Results

Fitness, nutrition, and mindset outcomes vary by individual and depend on factors outside our control, including your effort, consistency, and starting health status. We do not guarantee specific results.

5. Privacy

We collect the information you provide directly — such as your name, email, and any details you share through our contact form, booking scheduler, or intake process — to deliver and improve our coaching services. We do not sell your personal information.

Our site uses a small number of third-party services to operate: Stripe (payments), Calendly (scheduling), and Resend (email delivery). Each of these providers processes limited data — like your name, email, or payment details — solely to complete the function you're using (checkout, booking, or contact). We don't share your information with anyone beyond what's needed to provide the service you requested.

Questions about your data can be sent to hello@almafitandmind.com.
