The Signature Method

The Body–Plate–Mind Method

Every Alma Fit & Mind program is built around one belief: lasting transformation requires training the body, fueling it correctly, and rewiring the mindset that drives daily behavior.

01

Body

Individualized workout programming that builds strength, consistency, and confidence — scaled to your current fitness level, equipment, and lifestyle.

02

Plate

A realistic, personalized nutrition plan built around your preferences, schedule, and goals — never a one-size-fits-all crash diet.

03

Mind

1:1 coaching that addresses the habits, self-talk, and emotional patterns that determine whether a plan actually gets followed.

About the Founder

Meet Alma

Alma is a fitness and life coach with a distinctive combination of skills: hands-on expertise in strength training and exercise programming, applied knowledge of dieting and nutrition, and a genuine depth of understanding in mental health and mindset work.

Where most trainers stop at sets and reps, and most life coaches never touch programming or macros, Alma works across all three — because in practice, they're inseparable. Clients don't just receive a workout PDF or a meal plan; they receive a coach who helps them understand why they keep quitting, how to rebuild self-trust, and how to translate that mindset work into the gym and the kitchen.
